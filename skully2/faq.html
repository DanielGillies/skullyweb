What is the visor? Is it still the E-tint? Can we buy replacements?
Our visor is anti fog, anti scratch, anti glare, polarized, and has a photochromic tint. This means that the visor will automatically and naturally adjust from clear to tinted depending on the brightness outside. The photochromic visor also interacts with the Heads Up Display. As the HUD detects less light through the visor, the HUD would gradually adjust its brightness. Why we did not use an E-Tint visor: After failures in the field, the E-tint visor did not meet our high quality standards. There were issues with the button, the battery life, patchy tinting and how it interacted with the HUD. This did not meet our quality specs and needed to be engineered better. We needed a solution that could interact with the HUD and create a feedback loop. The photochromic visor was the best option for maximizing visibility of the HUD. This update will provide superior visibility and safety in a wider range of conditions for our customers. We're including this quality improvement to the AR-1 at no additional cost.

Cabon Fiber reinforced? What is it made of
SKULLY AR-1 is constructed of the high grade polycarbonate alloy shell, with a carbon fiber inlay and multi-density EPS. Paired with our laser cut comfort padding, this combination of materials enables AR-1 to exceed both DOT and ECE specifications and maximize serviceability.
