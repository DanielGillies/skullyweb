Careers

Join the Revolution

Engineering and design are our guiding principles.

  • Hardware

    Our mechanical and electrical engineers know that we are creating products for the everyday commuter. We know it is a harsh world out there and that wearable tech should be extremely robust. We want engineers who are excited about working in the lab and also push product development by testing in the field.

  • Software

    The SKULLY name represents a broader persona that emerges from the network of interconnectivity that our products create. We want a culture of developers that see the potential of this network and code to drive solutions that will amplify consumer experiences.

  • Design

    Our design process starts with understanding how key human factors and technology can come together seamlessly to increase the awareness of our customers’ surroundings. At SKULLY, we think augmented reality with a human factors focus can enhance your human experience and your ability to perceive your environment.

  • Business

    As we build our roadmap, we are asking ourselves “how do we apply technology to problems that are in need a solution that is uniquely augmented reality?” We wearable design should enhance our favorite experiences, making them richer and more memorable while using automation to create more time to do the things we love.
