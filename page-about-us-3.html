About Us

Skully pioneers advanced situational awareness technology. The company was founded in 2013 with the goal of revolutionizing the most frequent, ubiquitous, and dangerous aspect of our lives: transportation. Skully’s human-centered approach to design and technology has led to the development of the Skully AR-1, the first intelligent HUD motorcycle helmet for consumers. Skully is a privately held company with strategic partnerships spanning the world’s leading automotive technology companies.
