Opening and Closing the Visor

The visor has three distinct positions: closed, partially open, and fully open. Each is identifiable by an audible click and slight pause in downward or upward movement.

 

Measuring Your Head

With a measuring tape, begin about 1 inch (2.5 cm) above your eyebrows and wrap the tape all the way around. Compare your measurement to our Sizing Guide to get your correct helmet size.

 

Activating Voice Commands

When you say, “OK, SKULLY,” voice commands for GPS, music streaming, and calling are activated.

 

Turning the Helmet On and Off

Press the control button once to turn on the helmet. To turn it off, press and hold down the control button for at least six seconds.

 

Fastening and Releasing the Chinstrap

Secure and tighten the chinstrap using the ratchet mechanism. To release the chinstrap, pull the red tab on the quick-release buckle.

 

Adjusting the Heads-Up Display

The position of the Heads-Up Display can be adjusted vertically or laterally to achieve an optimal viewing position. You can also twist the HUD to angle its position.
