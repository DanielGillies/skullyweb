FAQ

  • Does the AR-1 come in my size?

    The SKULLY AR-1 is available in sizes S-XXL. To determine your size, please use the SKULLY Fit Guide below. The AR-1’s overall shape is similar to the Shoei RF-1200. If you know that you wear a size L Shoei helmet, we recommend ordering your SKULLY AR-1 one size larger because it is close-fitting. We will send you one additional premium padding set free of charge if your helmet feels slightly loose.

    Size Hat Size CM Inches
    Small 6 5/8 - 6 3/4 53 - 54 20 7/8 - 20 1/4
    Medium 6 7/8 - 7 55 - 56 21 5/8 - 22
    Large 7 1/8 - 7 1/4 57 - 58 22 1/2 - 22 7/8
    Extra Large 7 3/8 - 7 1/2 59 - 60 23 1/4 - 23 5/8
    XX Large 7 5/8 - 7 3/4 61 - 62 24 - 24 3/8

    When you receive your SKULLY AR-1, try it on and check the following:

    • • The inner lining is close-fitting (fits snugly all the way around your head)
    • • You cannot easily slide a finger along the side of your temple and into the helmet
    • • The cheek pads press up firmly against your cheeks
    • • The top pad presses against the top of your head

    If you can easily move the helmet around with your hands, it is too big. Please email our Customer Support team at connect@skully.com. We are committed to your safety and will rush ship you replacement premium padding to get you the perfect fit.

  • Is the AR-1 safety certified?

    The SKULLY AR-1 will ship DOT/ECE certified and is pending AS/NZS 1698:2006 certification. The ECE is the standard safety certification for the American Motorcycle Association, World Superbike Championship, MotoGP™, as well as the majority of independent racing organizations worldwide. With both DOT and ECE certifications, the SKULLY AR-1 has passed the toughest testing standards that exist for motorcycle helmets.

    What about Snell certification? ECE testing, the most rigorous and up-to-date standard in the world, is a combination of both DOT and Snell testing procedures. The ECE standard is also recognized by over 50 countries. We chose this route because ECE has a few extra tests added in, and we want to make sure the SKULLY AR-1 protects your noggin (and brain) for as many crash scenarios as possible. Research has shown that Snell helmets are associated with a higher rate of concussion and Traumatic Brain Injury in average crash scenarios compared to DOT/ECE helmets.

  • Will the HUD obstruct my view while riding?

    The heads up display (HUD) will NOT obstruct your view while riding. Here’s why:

    • The HUD is just outside your primary field of view (right hand side).
    • The HUD is seamless, appearing to float in the distance, which means you can always concentrate on the road ahead.
    • You can quickly see what’s happening behind you or GPS turn-by-turn visual cues because our patented optical technology ensures the that HUD is always in focus.
    • The HUD’s infinite focus allows you to view and comprehend information instantly without taking your eyes off the road.

  • What is the SKULLY limited warranty?

    The SKULLY AR-1 is covered under Limited Warranty for 12 months from the purchase date for the following:

    • • Defects in materials and workmanship of the AR-1 electronics hardware
    • • Defects in materials and workmanship of the original AR-1 firmware/software
    • • Battery capacity that has depleted 50% or more compared to original specifications

    If any of these issues occur, SKULLY will either repair the defect at no cost to you, or exchange the covered equipment for a new replacement.

    The SKULLY AR-1 Limited Warranty does NOT cover the following:

    • • Accidents such as drops, abuse, misuse, liquid contact, fire, earthquake damage, and external causes
    • • Damage that results from operating the helmet outside the permitted or intended use
    • • Damage from service performed by anyone who is not a SKULLY representative
    • • Lost or stolen helmets
    • • Cosmetic damage
    • • Consumable parts
    • • Preventable maintenance on covered equipment
    • • Defects caused by normal wear and tear and equipment aging

    If your helmet arrives damaged, you must notify SKULLY within 15 days of its arrival. If possible, we ask that you take pictures of the helmet and areas of damage before contacting us for assistance. We need as much information as possible to help you! Our complete Limited Warranty policy is always available for your reference.
