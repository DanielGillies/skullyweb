OUR STORY

If it Doesn't Exist, Make It.

Founded in 2013, SKULLY has revolutionized the transportation industry with a groundbreaking idea: advanced situational awareness technology can make riding safer, more efficient, and more enjoyable.

Ignite Interest, Break Records

The development of the SKULLY AR-1, the first smart motorcycle helmet with heads up display, broke pre-order campaign records, inspiring customers from 47 countries to buy the AR-1 ahead of its delivery date.

Listen to Feedback & Evolve

With worldwide acclaim from beta testers, industry experts, and media outlets, SKULLY and its top automotive technology partners are driven to push the boundaries of human-centered design.

Marcus Weller

Dr. Marcus Weller’s obsession with human-centered technology and design is the lifeblood of SKULLY. Marcus embraced the intelligence revolution with an innovative idea: that our basic sensory experiences could be enhanced to make motorcycling safer—make it extraordinary. With a background in intelligent transportation systems, human factors engineering, and industrial psychology, Marcus pioneered the development of the AR-1 helmet. His lifelong passion for riding and experience working in the automotive industry informs his unique approach to advanced situational awareness technology.

Mitch Weller

Mitchell Weller’s talent for business operations, product management, and material logistics helps propel SKULLY into new and existing markets. His passion for working on motorcycles keeps him grounded and, like his brother Marcus, in tune with the company’s steadfast dedication to enhancing rider safety through technology. With extensive experience as a human resources and operations specialist for the US Army, Mitchell is an energizing Chief of Staff. His leadership is crucial to the design, implementation, and management of SKULLY’s automation solutions.

Josh Bloom

Josh Bloom is a software designer, prototype expert, and leader who believes in disruptive, meaningful ideas. As SKULLY’s VP of Product Design, Josh leads multidisciplinary teams through cutting ­edge software development projects, providing technical guidance and cultivating an environment of creativity at every turn. His previous roles as UX Creative Director with Samsung and Associate Creative Director with frog, a global product strategy and design firm, honed his ability to harmonize technology with complex design processes.
