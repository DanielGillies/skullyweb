MISSION

  • VISION

    The idea actually came from a motorcycle accident that I had in Barcelona. A few months later I had this dream that I was back on the motorcycle that day. But there was one really important difference, which was that I had GPS maps and they were floating out in front of me like a hologram. - Marcus Weller

    Our iteritative prototype production process was born out of neccessity. Clearly Marcus' initial idea solved a major pain point for riders. Our early supporters can't be thanked enough, they gave us the strength to push past the challenges of prototyping and into mass production.

  • VALUES

    Our values were born out of the culture of riders. Radical self reliance, independant thought, [EMERSONIAN NATURE WORDS] being part of the world, and not viewing it from a bubble.

  • ENGINEER

    What began with an idea spawned the creation of a company, culture and methodology. Which has led us to the launch of the AR-1 the first interconnected helmet.
